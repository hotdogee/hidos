CellCloud

Instant cell image analysis without any parameter settings

CellCloud is a cloud-based cell image analysis service for modern biologists.

Quantify the experimental images is no more annoying and time counsuming, what we are doing is to save your precious time and speed up your research.

Features

These are the major advantages of CellCloud.

access_time

Real Time

You don’t need to wait for several hours or even days anymore. We process the image analysis in seconds, and capable to analysis multiple tasks simultaneously.

lightbulb_outline

No Parameters

We set all parameters automatically for you. You can easily analyze images and get the report without having any image analysis skills.

cloud_circle

Cloud Box

You do not only download the results, but also use our online manage system to check and manage your research findings anytime, anywhere.
